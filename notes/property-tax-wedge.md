## Mechanics [V]

- Ag dedication (ROH § 8-7.3): land dedicated to agricultural use assessed at
  **5% of FMV (1-yr dedication) or 1% of FMV (10-yr)**, taxed at the
  agricultural rate ($5.70/$1,000). Source:
  https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/honolulu/latest/honolulu/0-0-0-5936 ;
  https://realproperty.honolulu.gov/tax-relief-and-forms/dedications/agricultural-dedication/
- Industrial rate: $12.40/$1,000 on full FMV.

## The 2021 reclassification shock [V per Star-Advertiser 2021-11-16]

- Honolulu RPAD reclassified solar-farm land in the ag district as
  **industrial** ("power sold to Hawaiian Electric constitutes 'higher use'").
  Clearway's Waipio + Lanikuhana combined bills went **~$30,000 →
  ~$800,000/yr** (~25x), mid-PPA. Affected: Waipio 45 MW, Mililani 39 MW,
  Kawailoa 49 MW, others.
  https://www.staradvertiser.com/2021/11/16/hawaii-news/property-tax-assessments-jepoardize-oahus-clean-energy-projects/
- Council response: Bill 39 — 80% exemption for renewable projects; BFS
  intent: industrial class limited to the footprint in physical contact with
  equipment, balance stays agricultural. [ordinance number/current status
  UNVERIFIED — follow up; also Council testimony doc
  https://hnldoc.ehawaii.gov/hnldoc/document-download?id=12482]

## Analytical significance

1. A fourth restriction layer (after state ag statute, county LUO, PUC
   procurement): **fiscal treatment is discretionary and was repriced ex post
   by ~25x** — regulatory-risk premium on solar land deals; raises PPA bids.
2. Inverts Farm Bureau 2011 ("solar gives away tax resources"): counties
   capture MORE from solar. County fiscal self-interest should favor solar
   conversion — worth watching how county testimony aligns.
3. The wedge quantifies the ag-land subsidy the cap protects: dedicated ag
   land pays tax on 1–5% of FMV at $5.70/1000; solar pays on ~100% FMV at
   $12.40/1000 (pre-exemption) → per-acre tax ratio on the order of 40–250x
   depending on dedication tier and valuation change.
4. For the rents analysis: the dedication regime means landowners' carrying
   cost of HOLDING ag land (waiting for rezone/solar/development) is near
   zero — subsidized land banking; interacts with scarcity-rent hypothesis.
