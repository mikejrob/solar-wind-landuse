HB247 SD2       (?)


Measure Title: RELATING TO AGRICULTURAL LANDS.
Report Title: Agricultural Lands; Land Subdivisions; Condominium Property Regime
Description: Amends certain land subdivision and condominium property regime laws related to agricultural land, as recommended pursuant to Act 278, Session Laws of Hawaii 2019, to ensure certain condominium property regime projects within the agricultural district are used for agricultural purposes. (SD2)
Companion:  SB340
Package: None
Current Referral: WAL, AGR, JHA, FIN
