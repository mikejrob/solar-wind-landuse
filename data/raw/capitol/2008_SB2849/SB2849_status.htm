Measure Title:
RELATING TO AGRICULTURAL LANDS.
Report Title:
Agricultural-energy Facilities; Agricultural Districts
Description:
Permits the use of lands in agricultural land use districts to be used for agricultural-energy facilities provided that the primary activity of the agricultural-energy enterprise is agricultural activity. (CD1)
