FOREWORD
This publication contains a list of all of the bills passed by the Hawaii State
Legislature during the 2025 Regular Session which have become acts. We have included
the bill number, act number, title, and effective date. The first part of the report sets forth
the acts by bill number and the second part of the publication lists the acts by act number.
This publication and the publication Bills Vetoed and an Act with Vetoed Items, 2025 have
been generated to update the status information in the Supplemental Information Relating
to Bills Passed by the Hawaii State Legislature, Regular Session of 2025.
This publication has been created by the Legislative Reference Bureau Systems
Office. They are located at the State Capitol, Room 413 and their phone number is (808)
587-0700. Any questions covering the data shown may be directed to Lori Lee Ohta.

RELATING TO THE STABILIZATION OF PROPERTY INSURANCE.
Effective: This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025; provided that: (1)
The condominium loan revolving fund shall be abolished on June 30,
2047; (2) Any remaining balance in the condominium loan revolving
fund as of June 30, 2047, shall lapse to the credit of the general fund;
and (3) Any moneys received after June 30, 2047, for the repayment
of loans under the condominium loan program, including any interest
on the loan principal, shall be deposited into the general fund.
