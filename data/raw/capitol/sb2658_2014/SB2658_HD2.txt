Report Title:
Solar Energy; Agricultural Land

Description:
Allows solar energy facilities to occupy no more than 10% of a
parcel, or 20 acres of land, within agricultural lands with soil
classified as overall productivity rating class B or C if a
special use permit has been granted and the area occupied by the
solar energy facilities is also made available for compatible
agricultural activities. Requires that solar energy facilities
be decommissioned and removed within 12 months of the conclusion
of operation.   (5B2658 HD2)
