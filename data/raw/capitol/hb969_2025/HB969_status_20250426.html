HB969 HD2 SD2
Measure Title: RELATING TO WASTE OR DISPOSAL FACILITIES.
Report Title: Waste or Disposal Facilities; Prohibition; Significant Aquifers
Description: Prohibits the construction, modification, or expansion of any waste or disposal facility on land that is above a significant aquifer as determined by the Department of Health. (SD2)
Companion:
Package: None
Current Referral: EEP, WAL, JHA, FIN
