                APPLICANT KUIHELANI SOLAR PHASE 2, LLC'S
           MEMORANDUM RE KUIHELANI SOLAR PHASE 2 PROJECT
         STATE SPECIAL PERMIT APPLICATION FILED OCTOBER 20, 2025
      Comes now, Applicant KUIHELANI SOLAR PHASE 2, LLC (" Applicant"), an

affiliate of AES, by and through its attorneys MATSUBARA, KOTAKE & TABATA,

hereby submits its Memorandum Re Kuihelani Solar Phase 2 Project State Special

Permit Application filed October 20, 2025 ("Application").

       The Kuihelani Solar Phase 2 Project ("Project") is a 40-megawatt solar

photovoltaic system and a 160 megawatt-hour battery energy storage system, together

with a Project electrical substation, a Maui Electric Company, Ltd. switchyard and

interconnection lines, other ancillary facilities, and compatible agricultural activities.

       On May 26, 2026, the Maui Planning Commission held a hearing on the

Application and voted to recommend approval of the Application.

       The adoption hearing for the Findings of Fact, Conclusions of Law and Decision

and Order recommending approval of the Application is tentatively scheduled for July

14, 2026.

       The Land Use Commission ("LUC" or "Commission") hearing on the

Application is tentatively scheduled for August 19, 2026.

       The intent of this Memorandum is to file with the LUC a copy of the Application

to provide the public more time to review the Application, which is over 2,000 pages

long. Accordingly, the Application is attached hereto as Exhibit "A." It has been

confirmed to be ADA compliant so that it can be uploaded to the LUC website and

made available for review.
